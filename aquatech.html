Why Aquatech

Reliable, well-priced, brilliantly efficient

The efficient Toshiba compressor allows the Aquatech to operate efficiently in temperatures as low as -7°C. Available in sizes to suit small and large households, Aquatech has built a great reputation for reliability and customer satisfaction — backed by a 5-year warranty.

  • Compact, space-saving design
  • Advanced non-sacrificial impressed-current anode for superior protection in all water conditions
  • WiFi-enabled smart controller with five heating modes for flexible operation
  • Simple plug-in installation using a standard 10A power point
  • Integrated 1.8kW electric backup element for reliable heating
  • Operating volume: 43 dB(A) — ultra-quiet
Tank Options
