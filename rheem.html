Why Rheem

The trusted Aussie name, in heat-pump form

Rheem has been making Australian hot water systems for decades. The AmbiPower heat pump range brings that pedigree into the modern era — with high-performance R290 refrigerant, fast recovery times, and the kind of national service network you can't replicate.

  • Manufactured in Australia — Australian engineered for Australian conditions
  • National Rheem Service Network — ongoing service and aftercare confidence
  • R290 refrigerant across the range — future-proof, low GWP
  • 10-year cylinder warranty on all models
  • Touchscreen LED display for clear, intuitive control
The Range

Three configurations to fit your home

280L Glass Enamel All-in-One

For medium to large families of 4–6 people. Hardwired 2.4kW or 1.5kW plug-in backup element options.

  • Touchscreen LED display
  • Operating volume: 48 dB(A)
  • Recovery: 56 L/hr @ 19°C external
  • R290 refrigerant · 10-yr cylinder warranty

325L Vitreous Enamel Split

For larger households of 4–6 people. High recovery for high hot water demand.

  • Smart controller with touchscreen LED
  • Suits homes with narrow spaces
  • Operating volume: 47 dB(A)
  • Recovery: 87 L/hr @ 19°C external

315L Stainless Steel Split

Same high-recovery performance with corrosion-resistant stainless tank — no anode required.

  • Smart controller with touchscreen LED
  • Stainless tank for harsh conditions
  • Operating volume: 47 dB(A)
  • Recovery: 87 L/hr @ 19°C external
