FAQs

Common questions

How long does installation take?

Most heat pump installations are completed in a single day — usually 4–6 hours. Split-system installs (like Reclaim) can take a little longer. We'll always confirm timing with you upfront.

Do you handle the rebate paperwork?

Yes — completely. We're an approved installer for all federal and Victorian rebate schemes. You sign one form and we process everything, applying the value as a point-of-sale discount on your invoice.

What's covered by the warranty?

All systems we install come with a manufacturer's parts & labour warranty (typically 5–10 years). On top of that, we provide our own installation workmanship guarantee.

Can you replace a gas hot water system?

Absolutely — this is one of the most common jobs we do. With Victoria's gas phase-out from March 2027, we're helping homeowners switch ahead of the rush while rebates are highest.

What if I get a cheaper quote elsewhere?

We'll beat it. Our is real — show us a written quote from a licensed installer and we'll match or beat the total.
